# Disclaimer

Battery Fusion is a software layer built on top of Home Assistant.

It does NOT replace:
- battery BMS systems,
- inverter protections,
- electrical safety mechanisms.

Important:
- Accuracy depends on the quality of sensors and data.
- Incorrect configuration may lead to wrong conclusions.
- Always verify behavior in your system before relying on it.

Battery Fusion is designed to assist decision-making, not to replace core safety systems.

Use at your own responsibility.
